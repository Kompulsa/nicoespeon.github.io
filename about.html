Who am I?

My name is Nicolas Carlo. I’m a french student in an IT oriented business school in Paris. Some people knows me as espeon, especially across the web where I use this nickname.

I’m also a freelance web-developer.

I know back-end development with PHP/MySQL and some Ruby on Rails. I played with some framework such as CodeIgniter or Zend Framework 2. But I enjoy front-end development the most, keeping myself up-to-date with HTML, CSS and Javascript. I love building Backbone.js applications and improving my knowledge about front-end performance or CSS scalability (OOCSS, SMACSS, BEM, … all of that kind of things).
